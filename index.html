How The Virtual World May Change Reality

Metaverse virtual worlds are intriguing because they are a whole other reality. Each metaverse world is something that you can step into with the right technologies.
These might be virtual reality (VR) goggles that fully immerse you in that new world. Or, it could be augmented reality (AR) systems that overlay the metaverse onto the offline world.
But no matter how you look at it, the metaverse contains countless virtual worlds to explore.
